Hit Me Up

I always love learning new things.

When I was younger, I looked forward to every new day of class as an opportunity to learn. Outside the classroom, I explored whatever new skills I could find like stickfigure animation and graphic design.

I still carry with me this hunger for learning. Here are some of the things I'm working on learning.

Web Development

I've had a couple tries learning basic web development, but this time is the furthest I'm going, having learned basic front-end development. I am pursuing further studies to become a full-stack developer.

3D Modelling and Animation

I guess as an extension of my childhood fondness for stickfigure animation, I began learning all the tools Blender has to offer. I am practicing how to create my own 3D models from scratch, and am also looking forward to add 3D animation to my skillset.

UX and Graphic Design

I stumbled onto the magic of design a couple of years ago on YouTube. I was fascinated by just how deliberate and purposive design really is. It isn't just aesthetics. Since then, I've wanted to participate in the art of directing people's attention and improving their expriences.
