Hit Me Up

I had always wanted to make money.

Younger me was inspired by the visionary Steve Jobs. When I first learned about him, I knew that I wanted to make my own tech startup in the future. I wanted to be the one to provide people with the greatest and latest tech that completely disrupts current industries and improves lives.

Older me is less naive. That kind of life is difficult. Especially in the tech industry, where everything moves at GigaHertz speeds. But I still wnat it. I'm starting out smal by selling hydroponics systems, but eventually, I want to achieve my childhood dream of creating the next big tech thing.
